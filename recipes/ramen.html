Ramen Recipe

Easy ramen with eggs recipe

Ingredients

  • eggs
  • chicken stock
  • chicken meat
  • spinach
  • soy sauce
  • sriracha sauce
  • rice vinegar
  • garlic
  • ginger
  • ramen noodles

Toppings

  • dried seaweed
  • green onions
  • chile peppers
  • sriracha sauce

Steps

  1. In small pot boil water and boil eggs for 8min, cool and peel
  2. Combine chicken stock, chicken meat spinach, sauces, rice vinegar, garlic and ginger in pot. Simmer for 10min
  3. Cook ramen noodles for 3min
  4. Cover noodles with soup, add 1 soft boiled egg and garnish with seaweed, green onion chile peppers and sriracha sauce
